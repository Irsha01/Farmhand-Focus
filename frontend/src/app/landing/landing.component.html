About Us

Farmhandfocus, founded , is your go-to for all things agriculture. We provide high-quality inputs and services to farmers, using the latest tech and tools to ensure top-notch solutions..

  • Our approach is all about connecting with farmers in a meaningful way.
  • We build strong connections both backward and forward, creating a reliable pathway for products and services to reach rural India.
  • Our aim is to help farmers in reducing input cost by 20%-30%, 10-20% more yield and 30% to 40% more price for their farm produce.

Our Vision

When we talk about backward linkages, we're talking about things like helping farmers get access to seeds, modern machinery, crop insurance, and the right fertilizers and chemicals they need. .

Our Mission

Service to the farmers by providing quality agricultural inputs and services in fair price and help to get remunerative price for farm produce

Our Products

FARMING SOLUTIONS

Fertilizers

Farmhand Focus offers elaborate range of straight fertilizers, complex fertilizers and mixed fertilizers at one single place. We also provide range of micronutrient fertilizers, plant growth regulators and plant growth promotors.

Agrochemicals

Our crop protection products: Chemical pesticides (Insecticides, Fungicides, Herbicides and Bactericides), Bio pesticides and traps (Sticky traps, pheromone traps and solar light traps).

Seeds

High-quality Seeds of various field crops, horticultural crops and forage crops available in all our delivery centres.

Animal Feeds

Various brands of quality cattle feed, fish feed and poultry feed and poultry supplements supplied

Farm Machinery

Farm Mechanization emerges as an important technological intervention for bringing in the desired productivity push in the Agricultural sector. Machinery banks for small and marginal farmers can be the big game changers and provided by FarmHandFocus.

Market linkage for farm produce

Farmers can contact Delivery centres physically or contact via phone or access website for selling or storing their produce. Use combinations of sensors, IOT, block chain and ML driven data systems to create end-to-end traceability within the food chains.
